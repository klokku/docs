title: Garmin Watch App
description: How to enable Garmin Watch App integration in Klokku 
---

:::note[Only for managed version]
This feature is currently only available for Klokku on [app.klokku.com](https://app.klokku.com).
:::

There is an official Klokku App available on the Garmin Connect IQ App Store.

This app allows you to check and change your current budget item from your wrist.

[![Available on the Connect IQ Store](img/connect-iq_badge-dark.svg)](https://apps.garmin.com/apps/6691474b-4fc3-449f-9980-d937b9c7e333)
